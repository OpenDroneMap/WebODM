While we don't have a formal roadmap, we follow a bottom-up approach to decide what new features are added to WebODM. User feedback guides us in the decision making process and we collect such feedback.

We keep track of which features are being considered for inclusion with the [issue tracker](https://github.com/WebODM/WebODM/issues)

Don't see a feature that you want? [Open an issue](https://github.com/WebODM/WebODM/issues) or even better [a pull request](/contributing/).

Sometimes we also prioritize work that has received financial backing. If your organization is in the position to financially support the development of a particular feature, get in touch and we'll make it happen.
